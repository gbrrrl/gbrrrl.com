Rules for New Members

    • Promotion – We ask that you display a hyperlink (@ https://lostblognetwork.com/main) or badge for the network visibly on your blog's theme and/or in your blog's description, or links section. This, of course, promotes exposure to other members of our network.

    • Exclusivity – We ask that you remain exclusive to Lost Blog Network, and not join any other networks.

    • Association – We suggest, but don't enforce, following other blogs of interest within the network.

    • Username Change – We ask you to contact either @DISEASE or @FUNERAL if you change your URL, so we can promptly update your section on the network.

User Referrals

     All new members receive a single referral to potentially invite another blogger onto the network. You may send these referrals to either @DISEASE or @FUNERAL – both of which will make the final decision.

Dismissal

     At any time, if we feel your blog's content has shifted from its original focus, or your blog becomes inactive—you may be removed from the network without prior notice.
